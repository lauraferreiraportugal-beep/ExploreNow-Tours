Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I book a tour?

You can book a tour using our Booking page by filling out the form and selecting your preferred tour and date.

2. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, please contact us at info@explorenow.com at least 48 hours before your scheduled tour.

3. Are your tours suitable for children?

Yes, most of our tours are family-friendly. Please check each tour description for age recommendations.

4. Do I need to bring my own equipment?

For most tours, equipment is provided. Any special requirements will be listed in the tour description.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards and PayPal. Payments are made securely through our booking system.
